About Lincoln Air Conditioning

A locally owned HVAC company built on honest service, fair pricing, and showing up when we say we will.

Our Story

Lincoln Air Conditioning was founded in 2025 with a simple idea: every home and business in our city deserves fast, honest, professional HVAC service — without the upsells and runaround that give the industry a bad name.

Before starting the company, our founder spent over a decade in the field working for large regional HVAC contractors, learning the trade from the ground up and growing increasingly frustrated by commission-driven sales tactics. Lincoln Air Conditioning was built to do things differently: flat-rate pricing, certified technicians, and a team that treats every customer like a neighbor.

We're a new company, but not a new industry. Every technician on our team is certified and background-checked before they ever step into your home.
